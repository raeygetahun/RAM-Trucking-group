Our services

DEDICATED FLEET SERVICES

We partner with customers by providing dedicated fleet services. These services allow us to more efficiently utilize our equipment and driver pool. Efficiencies in utilization are returned to our customers through discounted rates without sacrificing premium service.

FLATBED TRANSPORTATION SERVICES

Our flatbed division offers a full range of services. We specialize in less-than-truckload (LTL) freight and brokerage. Our service offering includes, but are not limited to:
  • Full tarping for hauling steel and aluminum
  • Oversize and over-dimensional
  • Machinery & equipment
  • Lumber
